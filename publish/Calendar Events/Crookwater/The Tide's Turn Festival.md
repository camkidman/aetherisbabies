Every year (on Nymm 28) the poor section of Crookwater and the rich sectors get together to "celebrate" one thing: The Anniversary of [[The Scale Wall]]. They celebrate it for different reasons, though:

The Poor:
They rage. They have effigies and bonfires, they gather and sing songs that has been said will curse their enemies with enough fervor and voices.

The Rich:
They celebrate the day their own little 'country club' was built. When that wall was finished, they enacted law to squeeze every single dime out of the people in The Brine. And it worked. They keep those people in downright slavery and they're better off than ever because of it.
