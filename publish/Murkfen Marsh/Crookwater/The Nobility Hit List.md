## Countess Virelle Ashdawn, the Gilded Widow
### Riptide Code Name: Goldgrave
_Title:_ High Treasurer of The Vessel  
_Appearance:_ Pale gold skin (rumored to be from tinctures of powdered sunstone), crimson eyes, and a wardrobe made entirely of black mourning silks. Her jewelry never tarnishes because it’s cursed to drink the life of anyone who touches it.  
_Personality:_ Cold, meticulous, poetic in cruelty. Collects people like others collect coins.  
_Agenda:_ Oversees the city’s vaults and launders funding through “philanthropic restoration projects.”  
_Secret:_ She keeps her dead husband’s soul bound inside her ring finger, whispering financial counsel from beyond the grave.

## Lord Branik Toll, The Iron Saint

### Riptide Code Name: Saint Iron
_Title:_ Commander of the City Guard  
_Appearance:_ A war-scarred human with a silver breastplate polished to a mirror sheen, etched with the holy sigil of Ilmater—corrupted and inverted. His voice always carries, even in a whisper.  
_Personality:_ Devout zealot turned executioner. Believes mercy is weakness.  
_Agenda:_ Runs the guard as a private militia for The Vessel. Suppresses dissent, captures “heretics,” and oversees forced labor in the wall construction.  
_Secret:_ His armor is blessed with the blood of innocents; he hears their screams whenever he prays, and he _likes it._

##  Lady Calthea Venn, The Perfumed Knife

### Riptide Code Name: Jasmine
_Title:_ Minister of Culture and Festivals  
_Appearance:_ A drow noble with skin like obsidian glass and silver tattoos swirling over her neck and shoulders. Always smells of jasmine and steel.  
_Personality:_ Charismatic, savage, loves theater and murder equally.  
_Agenda:_ She uses city festivals as cover for dark rituals, feeding on collective joy.  
_Secret:_ She’s secretly undead herself—her laughter’s just breath echoing through a corpse animated by pure vanity.

##  Baron Renik Duskmire, The Bog-Blood Prince

### Riptide Code Name: Low Tide
_Title:_ Lord of Trade and Maritime Law  
_Appearance:_ Half-orc with seafoam eyes and damp, mossy hair that never dries. Always barefoot, trailing the scent of salt and rot.  
_Personality:_ Jovial on the surface, but his smile hides fangs—literally.  
_Agenda:_ Controls all shipping in and out of Crookwater, ensuring that The Vessel’s cargo moves unnoticed beneath the guise of trade.
